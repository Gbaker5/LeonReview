Sunrise & Sunset

Ever wanted to know what time the sun rises and sets at your dream vacation spot?
Well, look no further. This app will find your answer instantly.

So this is how it works.. Enter a latitude and longitude for your desired location
For example, heres a spot in Honolulu, Hawaii. (I did a quick google search to pinpoint a location....)
The latitude is: 21.276154 and the longitude is: -157.827140 .

The sunrise time for those coordinates is: 10:05:43 AM and the sunset time is: 9:24:49 PM
